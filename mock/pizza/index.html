Our Story

A family recipe,
28 years running.

Marco and Maria Conti moved from Naples to San Marcos in 1998. They brought one suitcase and one recipe book. Today, their two sons run the kitchen and Marco still makes the dough by hand every morning at 6am.

We don't cut corners. Our sauce simmers for four hours. Our dough ferments for two days. You'll taste the difference.

"The secret ingredient is always time."
— Marco Conti, Founder
