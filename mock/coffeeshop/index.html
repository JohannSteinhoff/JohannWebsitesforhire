Our Story

Coffee as a
daily practice.

Morning Ritual started in 2019 as a one-person pop-up at the San Marcos farmers market. Today we have a full café, a roasting program, and a community we didn't expect.

We believe great coffee starts at the source. We pay above fair-trade prices for all our beans, visit our farmers when we can, and never rush the roast.

"We're not just selling coffee. We're selling a moment in your morning."
